Privacy Policy

Charis — by Gigi Row
Last updated: 10 April 2026

1. Who we are

Charis ("Charis", "we", "us") is a creator analytics and management platform operated by Gigi Row, based in Australia. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

If you have any questions, contact us at gigirowplus@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

Account information

  • Email address (used for login and notifications)
  • Display name
  • Encrypted password (bcrypt-hashed; we never store plain passwords)
  • Account role (manager or creator)

Creator profile data

  • Creator name and contact details you choose to provide
  • Goals and growth targets you set
  • Notes and content ideas you create
  • Brand deal records you log

TikTok data (if you connect your account)

If you choose to connect a TikTok account via the official TikTok API, we may access:

  • Public profile information (username, display name, avatar, bio)
  • Follower count, following count, total likes
  • List of videos you have posted (titles, view counts, like counts, comment counts, share counts)
  • Aggregate video analytics (where permitted by TikTok)

We only access TikTok data after you explicitly authorise it through TikTok's OAuth flow. You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time from inside the app.

Usage data

  • Login timestamps
  • Pages visited within the app
  • Basic error logs (for debugging)

3. Why we collect this data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the Charis service (analytics, reports, content planning)
  • Display your creator metrics in dashboards
  • Generate growth roadmaps and performance predictions
  • Allow managers to track and support their creators
  • Send you notifications about deadlines, deals, and milestones (only if enabled)
  • Improve the service and fix bugs

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on encrypted servers hosted by DigitalOcean in their Sydney, Australia data centre. Database connections are TLS-encrypted. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12).

5. Who can see your data

  • You — full access to your own creator data
  • Your manager — if you are a creator with a manager assigned, they can see your data to provide management services
  • Gigi Row staff — limited access for technical support and bug fixing only, never for any other purpose

We do not share your data with anyone else without your explicit consent, unless legally required to do so.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — request a copy at any time
  • Correct inaccurate data through the in-app settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data as a JSON file (available in Settings → Danger Zone)
  • Withdraw consent for TikTok integration by disconnecting at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe your privacy rights have been breached

To exercise any of these rights, email gigirowplus@gmail.com.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently remove all your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g. financial records related to brand deals).

8. TikTok-specific terms

When you connect a TikTok account, you agree that:

  • We will only request the minimum scopes needed (basic profile, video list, stats)
  • We will store TikTok access tokens securely and refresh them as needed
  • We will not use your TikTok data for any purpose other than displaying it within Charis
  • We will delete cached TikTok data when you disconnect or delete your account
  • Your use of TikTok data is also governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy

9. Cookies and tracking

Charis uses session cookies only — these are necessary to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or advertising cookies.

10. Children's privacy

Charis is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

12. Contact us

Questions, concerns, or requests? Email gigirowplus@gmail.com.
